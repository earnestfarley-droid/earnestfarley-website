How engagements work

Diagnose. Build. Prove it holds.

01 — Diagnose

A short, fixed-scope assessment: where clinical and operational accountability actually sit, what the numbers say, and which constraint is structural versus behavioral.

02 — Build

The engagement itself: governance, operating cadence, adoption architecture, or turnaround execution, with your team doing the work alongside me so the capability stays.

03 — Prove it holds

The 90 for 90 standard applies to my own work: an engagement is complete when the key metrics hold for 90 consecutive days, not when the report is delivered.
