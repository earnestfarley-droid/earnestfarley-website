How to buy

Four ways in. One standard throughout.

Diagnostic Sprint

Fixed two-to-four weeks. On-site assessment, findings, and a prioritized roadmap. The lowest-commitment way to start and the natural entry point for any practice above.

Project Engagement

Defined scope, a named model, and milestone-based delivery. Best for a specific turnaround, consolidation, migration, or program build.

Fractional Retainer

A part-time senior operator on a monthly basis, embedded in the leadership team. Best for organizations that need the altitude without the full-time seat.

Interim Executive

Full-time, time-boxed leadership through a transition, a turnaround, or a search.

How engagements run

Diagnose. Build. Prove it holds.

01 · Diagnose

A short, fixed-scope assessment: where clinical and operational accountability actually sit, what the numbers say, and which constraint is structural versus behavioral.

02 · Build

The engagement itself: governance, operating cadence, adoption architecture, or turnaround execution, with your team doing the work alongside me so the capability stays.

03 · Prove it holds

The 90 for 90 standard applies to my own work: an engagement is complete when the key metrics hold for 90 consecutive days, not when the report is delivered.
